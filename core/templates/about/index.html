Our Story

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 1995 by a small team of passionate professionals, OB Global started as a local accounting firm with a vision to provide exceptional service and build lasting relationships with clients.

Expanding Our Reach

Through dedication and a commitment to excellence, we expanded our services and geographic reach, becoming a trusted advisor to businesses across industries and around the world.

Leading the Industry Today

Today, OB Global is a global professional services firm with a team of over 5,000 professionals across 20+ countries, helping organizations navigate complex challenges and achieve sustainable growth.
